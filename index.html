Welcome to wireworld, a sandbox where a few simple rules and some ingenuity can be used to create infinitely complex machines. But first, let's start with the basics. Click next to continue.

  • Empty cells stay empty.
  • Electron heads become electron tails.
  • Electron tails become wire segments
  • Wire segments become electron heads if they border either one or two other electron heads. Otherwise they stay as wire segments.
